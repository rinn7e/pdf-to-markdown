**Article 87.- (មាត្រា ៨៧.-)**
In case there is not yet an administrative court, the jurisdiction to adjudicate administrative cases shall be granted to the civil courts of the lower courts (សាលាជម្រះក្តីជាន់ទាប) and the Civil Chamber of the higher courts.

**Article 88.- (មាត្រា ៨៨.-)**
When there are not yet commercial advisors or labour advisors, the composition for adjudicating commercial cases and labour cases shall implement the provisions on civil procedure.

**Article 89.- (មាត្រា ៨៩.-)**
When there are not yet provisions on commercial procedure, the adjudication of commercial cases shall implement the provisions on civil procedure and other legal provisions in force.

When there are not yet provisions on labour procedure, the adjudication of labour cases shall implement the provisions on civil procedure and other legal provisions in force.

When there are not yet provisions on administrative procedure, the adjudication of administrative cases shall implement the provisions on civil procedure and other legal provisions in force.

**Article 90.- (មាត្រា ៩០.-)**
In the first 05 (five) years after this law enters into force, the appointment of heads of administrative units of courts at all levels can be implemented according to actual qualification conditions.

 The Director of Clerks (នាយកក្រឡាបញ្ជី) of the First Instance Court can be appointed as the head or deputy head of the Administrative Secretariat of the First Instance Court where they are currently working.

The Director of Clerks of the higher courts can be appointed as the Secretary General or Deputy Secretary General of the General Secretariat of Administration of the higher courts where they are currently working.
