## Section 3 (ផ្នែកទី ៣)
## General Secretariat of Administration of the Appeal Court (អគ្គលេខាធិការដ្ឋានរដ្ឋបាលសាលាឧទ្ធរណ៍)

**Article 52.- (មាត្រា ៥២.-)**
The General Secretariat of Administration of the Appeal Court is the chief of staff to the Appeal Court and the General Prosecution Office attached to the Appeal Court on:
- Personnel, administrative, and financial work.
- Court administrative management work for technical assistance to the work programs of the Appeal Court and the General Prosecution Office attached to the Appeal Court.
- Budget project preparation work for the President of the Appeal Court and the Prosecutor General attached to the Appeal Court to review and decide.
- Implementing budget work according to the approved projects.
- Regularly reporting on personnel, administrative, and financial work to the President of the Appeal Court and the Prosecutor General attached to the Appeal Court.
- Public relations work.
- Periodic submission of verdicts (សាលដីកា) of the Appeal Court to the Ministry of Justice.
- Preparing and submitting monthly, quarterly, semi-annual, and annual personnel, administrative, and financial work reports to the Ministry of Justice.
- Other work assigned by the President of the Appeal Court and the Prosecutor General attached to the Appeal Court.
- Implementing other tasks as defined by legal instruments.

The General Secretariat of Administration of the Appeal Court is divided into departments (នាយកដ្ឋាន) led by one head and several deputy heads as necessary.

The organization and functioning of the General Secretariat of Administration of the Appeal Court shall be defined by Sub-decree upon request from the Minister of Justice.

**Article 53.- (មាត្រា ៥៣.-)**
The General Secretariat of Administration of the Appeal Court is led by one Secretary General (អគ្គលេខាធិការ) and several Deputy Secretaries General (អគ្គលេខាធិការរង) as necessary. The Secretary General shall be appointed by Royal Decree upon request reported by the Prime Minister. The Minister of Justice consults with the President of the Appeal Court and the Prosecutor General attached to the Appeal Court.
