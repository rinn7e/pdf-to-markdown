# Chapter 1 (ជំពូកទី ១)
# General Provisions (បទប្បញ្ញត្តិទូទៅ)

**Article 1.- (មាត្រា ១.-)**
This Law aims to:
- Guarantee the independence of the judicial power (ធានាឯករាជ្យនៃអំណាចតុលាការ).
- Guarantee the impartiality (អនាគតិ) and protect the rights and freedoms of citizens.
- Guarantee the good functioning of the judicial organizations (អង្គការតុលាការ) and the prosecution organizations (អង្គការអយ្យការ).
- Promote public services to be efficient and fast.
- Guarantee the provision of justice in all types of cases to increase the confidence of citizens and contribute to strengthening social safety.

**Article 2.- (មាត្រា ២.-)**
This Law has the objective to:
- Define the organization and functioning of judicial organizations in all sectors and at all levels which are the adjudicating courts (សាលាជម្រះក្តី) of the Kingdom of Cambodia.
- Define the organization and functioning of the prosecution organizations (អង្គការអយ្យការ) attached to the adjudicating courts at all levels of the Kingdom of Cambodia.
- Define the jurisdiction (សមត្ថកិច្ច) of the courts in all sectors and at all levels according to specialization.
- Organize the management work, administrative work, and the functioning process of the courts.

**Article 3.- (មាត្រា ៣.-)**
The adjudicating courts (សាលាជម្រះក្តី) of the Kingdom of Cambodia include:
- First Instance Courts (សាលាដំបូង)
- Appeal Courts (សាលាឧទ្ធរណ៍)
- Supreme Court (តុលាការកំពូល).

The First Instance Court is the lower-level court (សាលាជម្រះក្តីជាន់ទាប). The Appeal Court and the Supreme Court are the higher-level courts (សាលាជម្រះក្តីជាន់ខ្ពស់).

Based on actual necessary needs, the establishment of any adjudicating court or other specialized adjudicating courts (សាលាជម្រះក្តីឯកទេស) shall be made by law.
