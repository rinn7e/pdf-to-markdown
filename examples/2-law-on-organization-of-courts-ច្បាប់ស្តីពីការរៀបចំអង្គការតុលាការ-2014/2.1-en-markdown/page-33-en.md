**Article 81.- (មាត្រា ៨១.-)**
Previous provisions related to the Military Court and the Prosecution Office attached to the Military Court, as well as provisions on jurisdiction and procedure applied for the processing of military offense cases, shall continue to have the force of implementation after this law enters into force until there are new provisions to replace them.

**Article 82.- (មាត្រា ៨២.-)**
While waiting for the putting into operation of other Appeal Courts, the current Appeal Court located in Phnom Penh has territorial jurisdiction extending over the entire territory of the Kingdom of Cambodia.

**Article 83.- (មាត្រា ៨៣.-)**
The putting into operation of various specialized courts of the First Instance Court as stated in Article 14 of this law shall be done by Prokas of the Minister of Justice.

**Article 84.- (មាត្រា ៨៤.-)**
In case the First Instance Court of any province has not yet been put into operation or cannot be put into operation, the jurisdiction to adjudicate all cases in that province shall be granted to any First Instance Court by Royal Decree.

**Article 85.- (មាត្រា ៨៥.-)**
When the Commercial Chamber and the Labour Chamber of the higher courts (សាលាជម្រះក្តីជាន់ខ្ពស់) cannot operate due to a lack of judges, the adjudication of commercial cases and labour cases is the jurisdiction of the Civil Chamber of the higher courts. These tasks shall be managed by the President of the higher court where the specialized chamber is located.

When any specialized chamber above operates in accordance with this law, other specialized chambers must transfer cases that are not within their jurisdiction and have not yet been handled back to the specialized chamber that has jurisdiction.

**Article 86.- (មាត្រា ៨៦.-)**
In case there are not yet enough judges, an investigating judge can continue to fulfill the function as an adjudicating judge, but cannot adjudicate a case which they investigated.
