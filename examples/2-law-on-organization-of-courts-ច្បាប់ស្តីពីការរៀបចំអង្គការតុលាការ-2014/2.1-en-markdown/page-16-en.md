## ផ្នែកទី ៣
## Administrative Secretariat of the First Instance Court (លេខាធិការដ្ឋានរដ្ឋបាលសាលាដំបូង)

**Article 32.- (មាត្រា ៣២.-)**
The Administrative Secretariat of the First Instance Court is the chief of staff to the First Instance Court and the Prosecution Office attached to the First Instance Court on:
- Personnel, administrative, and financial work.
- Court administrative management work for technical assistance to the work programs of the First Instance Court and the Prosecution Office attached to the First Instance Court.
- Budget project preparation work for the President of the First Instance Court and the Prosecutor to review and decide.
- Regularly reporting on personnel, administrative, and financial work to the President of the First Instance Court and the Prosecutor.
- Implementing budget work according to the approved projects.
- Public relations work.
- Periodic submission of judgments (សាលក្រម) of the First Instance Court to the Ministry of Justice.
- Preparing and submitting monthly, quarterly, semi-annual, and annual personnel, administrative, and financial work reports to the Ministry of Justice.
- Other work assigned by the President of the First Instance Court and the Prosecutor of the Prosecution Office attached to the First Instance Court.
- Implementing other tasks as defined by legal instruments.

The Administrative Secretariat of the First Instance Court shall be divided into offices (ការិយាល័យ) according to actual necessary numbers. An office is led by one head (ប្រធាន) and several deputy heads (អនុប្រធាន) as necessary.

The organization and functioning of the Administrative Secretariat of the First Instance Court shall be defined by Sub-decree upon request from the Minister of Justice.

**Article 33.- (មាត្រា ៣៣.-)**
The Administrative Secretariat of the First Instance Court is led by one head and several deputy heads as necessary. The Head of the Secretariat shall be appointed by Sub-decree upon request from the Minister of Justice after consulting with the President of the First Instance Court and the Prosecutor of the Prosecution Office attached to the First Instance Court.

The officials working at the Secretariat below the rank of the Head of the Secretariat shall be appointed by Prokas (ប្រកាស) of the Minister of Justice.
