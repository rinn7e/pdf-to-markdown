Labour advisors do not fulfill their duties permanently in the Labour Chamber of the Appeal Court. Labour advisors shall come to fulfill their duties upon invitation from the President of the Labour Chamber of the Appeal Court only.

The modalities of appointment and the fulfillment of duties of labour advisors shall be defined by Sub-decree upon request from the Minister of Justice after consulting with the Minister in charge of the labour sector.

**Article 48.- (មាត្រា ៤៨.-)**
A joint chamber (សភារួមក្រុម) may be established to operate by a decision (ដីកាសម្រេច) of the President of the Appeal Court on their own initiative or upon request from any president of a chamber when any case raises an issue that falls under the jurisdiction of many chambers or if that issue has received conflicting solutions (ដំណោះស្រាយខ្វែងគ្នា) before different chambers of the same Appeal Court. The joint chamber operates upon invitation of the President of the Appeal Court.

 The decision of the joint chamber regarding jurisdiction between various chambers and legal disputes has binding power (អំណាចបង្ខំ) for all chambers of the Appeal Court to follow.

When a joint chamber is established, this chamber must have a composition of at least 05 (five) judges who are equally divided among the judges representing the relevant chambers. The joint chamber is under the presidency of the President of the Appeal Court.

The President of the Appeal Court issues a decision in accordance with the decision of the joint chamber. The decision on jurisdictional disputes and legal disputes is a final decision (សេចក្តីសម្រេចបិទផ្លូវតវ៉ា).

## Section 2 (ផ្នែកទី ២)
## General Prosecution Office attached to the Appeal Court (មហាអយ្យការអមសាលាឧទ្ធរណ៍)

**Article 49.- (មាត្រា ៤៩.-)**
Attached to the Appeal Court, there is a General Prosecution Office (មហាអយ្យការ) which has the following composition:
- Prosecutor General (អគ្គព្រះរាជអាជ្ញា)
- Deputy Prosecutor General (អគ្គព្រះរាជអាជ្ញារង)
- Prosecutor (ព្រះរាជអាជ្ញា)
- Deputy Prosecutor (ព្រះរាជអាជ្ញារង)
- Court clerk (ក្រឡាបញ្ជី) and administrative officers (មន្ត្រីរដ្ឋបាល), and may have other legal specialized officers who work at the General Prosecution Office attached to the Appeal Court.
