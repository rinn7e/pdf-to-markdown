The Head and Deputy Head of the Secretariat shall be selected from civil servants who have at least 05 (five) years of work experience in legal, administrative, or financial specializations. The Head and Deputy Head of offices shall be selected from civil servants who have at least 03 (three) years of work experience in legal, administrative, or financial specializations.

All officials working at the Secretariat, including the Head of the Secretariat, must have their framework (ក្របខណ្ឌ) at the Ministry of Justice.

**Article 34.- (មាត្រា ៣៤.-)**
The general assembly (សន្និបាត) of the First Instance Court takes place under the presidency of the President of the First Instance Court and the Prosecutor of the Prosecution Office attached to the First Instance Court to summarize past work results and define the administrative work direction of the First Instance Court for the following year. The general assembly report of the First Instance Court shall be sent to the Ministry of Justice.

# Chapter 3 (ជំពូកទី ៣)
# Appeal Courts and General Prosecution Offices attached to Appeal Courts (សាលាឧទ្ធរណ៍ និងមហាអយ្យការអមសាលាឧទ្ធរណ៍)

## Section 1 (ផ្នែកទី ១)
## Appeal Courts (សាលាឧទ្ធរណ៍)

### Sub-section 1 (អនុផ្នែកទី ១)
### Organization and Functioning of Appeal Courts (ការរៀបចំ និងការប្រព្រឹត្តទៅរបស់សាលាឧទ្ធរណ៍)

**Article 35.- (មាត្រា ៣៥.-)**
The Appeal Court is the second-level adjudicating court.

Appeal Courts include the Phnom Penh Appeal Court and Regional Appeal Courts (សាលាឧទ្ធរណ៍តំបន់). Putting each Regional Appeal Court into operation and defining the territorial jurisdiction of these Regional Appeal Courts shall be done by Royal Decree.

**Article 36.- (មាត្រា ៣៦.-)**
The composition of an Appeal Court includes:
- President (ប្រធាន)
- Vice-president (អនុប្រធាន)
- Judge (ចៅក្រម)
- Court clerk (ក្រឡាបញ្ជី) and administrative officers (មន្ត្រីរដ្ឋបាល), and may have other legal specialized officers who work at the Appeal Court.
