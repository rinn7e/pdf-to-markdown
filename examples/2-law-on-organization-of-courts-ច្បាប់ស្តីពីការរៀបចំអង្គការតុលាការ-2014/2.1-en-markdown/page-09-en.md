At the level of the Supreme Court, this administrative unit is called the General Secretariat of Administration of the Supreme Court (អគ្គលេខាធិការដ្ឋានរដ្ឋបាលតុលាការកំពូល), having a rank equal to a General Department (អគ្គនាយកដ្ឋាន).

**Article 11.- (មាត្រា ១១.-)**
The Ministry of Justice has the right to oversee the administrative affairs of all adjudicating courts, with a General Department of Court Administration (អគ្គនាយកដ្ឋានកិច្ចការរដ្ឋបាលតុលាការ) as the chief of staff. The Ministry of Justice may issue legal instruments (លិខិតបទដ្ឋានគតិយុត្ត) and various necessary instructions to ensure the better functioning of court administrative management at all levels.

In case of necessity, the Minister of Justice may assign an inspection (អធិការកិច្ច) on any specific issue, and then report to the Supreme Council of Magistracy (ឧត្តមក្រុមប្រឹក្សានៃអង្គចៅក្រម) for review and decision.

Higher-level adjudicating courts have the jurisdiction to oversee the adjudication affairs (កិច្ចការជម្រះក្តី) of lower-level adjudicating courts. This oversight takes place within the framework of remedy procedures (នីតិវិធីឧបាស្រ័យ) in accordance with the provisions on procedures in force.

# Chapter 2 (ជំពូកទី ២)
# First Instance Courts and Prosecution Offices attached to First Instance Courts (សាលាដំបូង និងអយ្យការអមសាលាដំបូង)

## Section 1 (ផ្នែកទី ១)
## First Instance Courts (សាលាដំបូង)

### Sub-section 1 (អនុផ្នែកទី ១)
### Organization and Functioning of First Instance Courts (ការរៀបចំ និងការប្រព្រឹត្តទៅរបស់សាលាដំបូង)

**Article 12.- (មាត្រា ១២.-)**
The First Instance Court is the first-level adjudicating court which must be established in Phnom Penh and in every province of the Kingdom of Cambodia. Putting various First Instance Courts into operation shall be done by Royal Decree.

The First Instance Court shall be divided into specialized courts. The First Instance Court is a general adjudicating court (សាលាជម្រះក្តីទូទៅ) which has the jurisdiction to adjudicate all cases, except for cases which are under the jurisdiction of special adjudicating courts or extraordinary adjudicating courts (សាលាជម្រះក្តីវិសាមញ្ញ) established by separate laws.

The composition of the First Instance Court includes:
- President (ប្រធាន)
- Vice-president (អនុប្រធាន)
- Judge (ចៅក្រម).
