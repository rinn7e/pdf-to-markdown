**Article 78.- (មាត្រា ៧៨.-)**
Unless there are provisions on procedures stating otherwise, all parties in a case and persons who have an interest in a case, including the representative of the prosecution, can file a motion for nullity (ប្តឹងសុំមោឃកម្ម) against a judgment or verdict of a court due to a violation of the rules on incompatibility as stated in Article 74, Article 75, Article 76, and Article 77 above.

This motion for nullity can be made at any time before the court in the recourse procedure (នីតិវិធីឧបាស្រ័យ) by implementing the provisions on procedures in force. Even if that judgment or verdict has become final (ស្ថាពរ), the motion for nullity can still be made within 60 (sixty) days from the date of knowing about the grounds stated in Article 74, Article 75, Article 76, and Article 77 above after the judgment or verdict has become final. In the latter case, the motion for nullity must be filed at the court that issued the final judgment or verdict, but there must be a different composition of adjudicating judges to review and decide on the nullity of that final judgment or verdict and to implement further procedures in accordance with the provisions in force.

# Chapter 6 (ជំពូកទី ៦)
# Budgets of Courts and Prosecution Offices attached to Courts (ថវិកាសាលាជម្រះក្តី និងអយ្យការអមសាលាជម្រះក្តី)

**Article 79.- (មាត្រា ៧៩.-)**
The First Instance Court and the Prosecution Office attached to the First Instance Court, the Appeal Court and the General Prosecution Office attached to the Appeal Court, as well as the Supreme Court and the General Prosecution Office attached to the Supreme Court, have separate budget packages for operation, which are located within the budget package of the Ministry of Justice.

The President of the court at all levels is an orderer (អាណាប័ក) delegated by the Minister of Justice.

The management and allocation of the budgets of courts and prosecution offices attached to courts as defined in the 1st paragraph above shall be defined by Sub-decree.

# Chapter 7 (ជំពូកទី ៧)
# Inter-provisions (អន្តរប្បញ្ញត្តិ)

**Article 80.- (មាត្រា ៨០.-)**
Courts at all levels as well as prosecution organizations attached to these courts which were established before this law enters into force shall continue their operation in accordance with the provisions of this law.
