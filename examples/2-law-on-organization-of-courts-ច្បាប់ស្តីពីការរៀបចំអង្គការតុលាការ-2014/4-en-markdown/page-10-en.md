- Vice-president (អនុប្រធាន)
- Judge (ចៅក្រម)
- Court clerk (ក្រឡាបញ្ជី)
- Administrative officers (មន្ត្រីរដ្ឋបាល) and may have other legal specialized officers who work at the First Instance Court.

**Article 14.- (មាត្រា ១៤.-)**
Each First Instance Court is divided into specialized courts as follows:
- Civil Court (តុលាការរដ្ឋប្បវេណី)
- Criminal Court (តុលាការព្រហ្មទណ្ឌ)
- Commercial Court (តុលាការពាណិជ្ជកម្ម)
- Labour Court (តុលាការការងារ).

Other specialized courts of the First Instance Court may be established based on necessary needs. This establishment shall be done by Royal Decree.

Each specialized court of the First Instance Court issues its decisions autonomously (ស្វ័យភាព) within its jurisdiction in the name of the First Instance Court where that specialized court is located.

**Article 15.- (មាត្រា ១៥.-)**
The composition of each specialized court includes:
- President of the Court (ប្រធានតុលាការ)
- Judge (ចៅក្រម)
- Court clerk (ក្រឡាបញ្ជី).

The president of each specialized court, including judges and court clerks, shall be appointed by the President of the First Instance Court. The process of these specialized courts shall be defined by the President of the First Instance Court in accordance with the legal instruments in force.

**Article 16.- (មាត្រា ១៦.-)**
Each specialized court of the First Instance Court adjudicates by a single judge (ចៅក្រមទោល) or by a trial chamber (ក្រុមប្រឹក្សាជំនុំជម្រះ) in accordance with the provisions on civil procedure, criminal procedure, or other provisions on procedures in force.

In case any judge has a task (ធុរៈ) or is in a situation of being unable to fulfill duties, that judge may be substituted by a judge in the subsequent order specified in the annual work allocation table of the President of the First Instance Court.
