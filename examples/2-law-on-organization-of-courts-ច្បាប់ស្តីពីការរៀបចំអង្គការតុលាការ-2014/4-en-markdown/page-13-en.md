**Article 21.- (មាត្រា ២១.-)**
The Civil Court of the First Instance Court has the jurisdiction to adjudicate civil cases and all cases for which there is no provision granting jurisdiction to other specialized courts by implementing the provisions on civil procedure.

The Civil Court of the First Instance Court adjudicates civil cases by a single judge or by a trial chamber by implementing the provisions on civil procedure.

**Article 22.- (មាត្រា ២២.-)**
The Commercial Court of the First Instance Court has the jurisdiction to adjudicate commercial cases, including bankruptcy cases (រឿងក្តីក្ស័យធន) in commercial matters by implementing the provisions on commercial procedure.

**Article 23.- (មាត្រា ២៣.-)**
The Commercial Court adjudicates with 03 (three) judges accompanied by 02 (two) advisors (ទីប្រឹក្សា) who are merchants or persons with knowledge of commercial law in the case of commercial cases where the object of the lawsuit has a value from 1,000,000,000 (one billion) Riels upward.

The Commercial Court adjudicates with 01 (one) judge accompanied by 02 (two) advisors who are merchants or persons with knowledge of commercial law in the case of commercial cases where the object of the lawsuit has a value from 100,000,000 (one hundred million) Riels to below 1,000,000,000 (one billion) Riels.

The Commercial Court adjudicates with 01 (one) judge without the need for participation from commercial advisors in the case of commercial cases where the object of the lawsuit has a value below 100,000,000 (one hundred million) Riels.

The judgment (សាលក្រម) of the Commercial Court shall be decided by 01 (one) judge or 03 (three) judges according to paragraphs 1 and 2 above after consulting with the commercial advisors.

Commercial advisors do not fulfill their duties permanently (អចិន្ត្រៃយ៍) in the Commercial Court of the First Instance Court. Commercial advisors shall come to fulfill their duties at the First Instance Court upon invitation from the President of the Commercial Court of the First Instance Court.

**Article 24.- (មាត្រា ២៤.-)**
The modalities of appointment and the fulfillment of duties of commercial advisors shall be defined by Sub-decree (អនុក្រឹត្យ) upon request from the Minister of Justice after consulting with the Minister of Commerce.
