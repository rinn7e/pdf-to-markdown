**Article 56.- (មាត្រា ៥៦.-)**
The composition of the Supreme Court includes:
- President (ប្រធាន)
- Vice-president (អនុប្រធាន)
- Judge (ចៅក្រម)
- Court clerk (ក្រឡាបញ្ជី) and administrative officers (មន្ត្រីរដ្ឋបាល), and may have other legal specialized officers who work at the Supreme Court.

**Article 57.- (មាត្រា ៥៧.-)**
The Supreme Court has specialized chambers (សភាជំនាញ) as follows:
- Criminal Chamber (សភាព្រហ្មទណ្ឌ)
- Civil Chamber (សភារដ្ឋប្បវេណី)
- Commercial Chamber (សភាពាណិជ្ជកម្ម)
- Labour Chamber (សភាការងារ).

In case of necessity, other specialized chambers of the Supreme Court may be established by Royal Decree.

Each chamber issues its decisions autonomously in the name of the Supreme Court.

**Article 58.- (មាត្រា ៥៨.-)**
The composition of each chamber includes:
- President of the Chamber (ប្រធានសភា)
- Judge (ចៅក្រម) and
- Court clerk (ក្រឡាបញ្ជី).

The president of each chamber is appointed by the President of the Supreme Court. The number of judges, court clerks, and the process of these chambers shall be defined by the President of the Supreme Court.

**Article 59.- (មាត្រា ៥៩.-)**
Each chamber of the Supreme Court adjudicates with 05 (five) judges, one of whom is the president, in accordance with the provisions on procedures in force.
